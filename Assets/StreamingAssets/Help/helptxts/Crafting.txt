Open your inventory with <b>E</b>
Place the crafting ingredients in the crafting slots. The positions don't matter as long as they are in the crafting slots.
If you can craft something with the items in the crafting slots, the craftable item will be shown directly to the right. Click it to craft it in return for the ingredients required.
Hint: right now, the only recipie is an axe, which requires 3 sticks, 1 rock, and 1 slime goo.
